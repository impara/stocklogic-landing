Multi-location bundle FAQ

Does StockLogic change my orders?

No. StockLogic uses Shopify inventory mutations and does not add component lines or dummy items to the order. Taxes, revenue, and the customer-facing order remain unchanged.

What happens when two inventory updates arrive together?

StockLogic serializes updates for the same bundle and location, verifies the current Shopify quantity before writing, and recalculates when that quantity has changed.

Does multi-location inventory follow Shopify routing?

Yes for Shopify-managed locations. StockLogic uses Shopify fulfillment orders to apply component deductions at the assigned location and reconciles moves and splits without decrementing every warehouse.

What happens if the MBQ calculation fails?

StockLogic fails closed instead of guessing a warehouse or silently using partial location data. The failed operation is retained for retry and operational alerting.

Are third-party fulfillment and 3PL locations supported?

Not for inventory adjustments. StockLogic detects those assignments and stops without changing stock. Route StockLogic bundles through Shopify-managed locations.
