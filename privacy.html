Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2025

StockLogic is committed to protecting your data and your customers' privacy. This policy explains how we handle information.

1. Data We Collect

We process order and inventory data from your Shopify store to perform synchronization. This includes SKUs, stock levels, and order line items.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively for inventory management. We do not sell or share customer data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices to ensure your store information remains secure.

4. Data Deletion

When you uninstall the app, we purge all store-related data within 48 hours in compliance with Shopify's requirements.
